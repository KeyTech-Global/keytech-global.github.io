About KeyTech

Transforming academic knowledge into real-world technology solutions since 2025

KeyTech is a technology-focused startup founded by a team of six B.Sc. Computer Science students. Conceptualized in 2025 and implemented in 2026, we bridge the gap between academic learning and practical implementation.

Student-Powered Innovation: We combine cutting-edge academic knowledge with practical development skills to create solutions that are both innovative and production-ready.
